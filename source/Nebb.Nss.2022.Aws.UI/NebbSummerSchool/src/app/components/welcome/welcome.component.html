FAQ

How can I manage and view my media files?

The Media Library app allows you to list and sort all your media files, including audio, video, and images. You can also query your media files and preview them quickly for your convenience.

Can I delete my media files and restore them later if needed?

Yes, you can soft delete your media files in the app, and they will be moved to the Archive. From there, you can easily revert the deletion and restore your files if needed.

How scalable is the Media Library app?

We have ensured that the solution is highly scalable and can handle up to 1000 uploads per minute. This ensures that the app can accommodate the needs of its users, even as it continues to grow and expand.
